# Funding Strategy & Capital Stack

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01  
**Target:** €420M over 5 years

---

## Executive Summary

INOS/SSOT/HEMPIN will be funded through a diversified capital stack prioritizing non-dilutive, mission-aligned funding. The target is 57% grants, 22% private capital, 21% revenue.

| Source | Target % | 5-Year Target (€) | Dilution | Mission Alignment |
|--------|----------|-------------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Grants (EU, UN, etc.)** | 57% | €240M | 0% | Very High |
| **Private Capital** | 22% | €92M | 20-30% | Medium |
| **Revenue** | 21% | €88M | 0% | High |
| **TOTAL** | **100%** | **€420M** | **20-30%** | **High** |

---

## Why This Capital Stack?

### Traditional Startup Model (Rejected)

Typical Tech Startup:
├── Seed: €2M (15% dilution) |
├── Series A: €10M (20% dilution) |
├── Series B: €30M (20% dilution) |
├── Series C: €50M (20% dilution) |
├── Series D: €100M (15% dilution) |
└── TOTAL: €192M raised, ~70% founder dilution |


**Problem:** Founders lose control, investors demand exits, mission drifts.

### INOS/SSOT Model (Selected)

Mission-Aligned Stack:
├── Grants: €240M (0% dilution)
├── Private Capital: €92M (20-30% dilution)
├── Revenue: €88M (0% dilution)
└── TOTAL: €420M, ~25% founder dilution


**Benefit:** Founders retain control, mission locked, sustainable long-term.

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation | Trigger |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Grant rejection** | Diversify across 500+ sources | <25% success rate |
| **Payment delays** | 12-month cash buffer | Cash <6 months |
| **Compliance burden** | Dedicated compliance team | Any audit finding |
| **Political changes** | Multi-region diversification | Any single region >50% |
| **Currency fluctuations** | Hedge major currencies | >20% revenue in USD |
| **Mission drift** | Foundation structure, mission lock | Any investor pressure |
