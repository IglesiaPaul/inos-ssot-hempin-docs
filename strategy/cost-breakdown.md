## 🛡️ Financial Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation | Trigger Point |
|------|------------|---------------|
| **Revenue below target** | 18-month runway in treasury | <80% of plan for 2 quarters |
| **Grant application failure** | Diversify across 500+ sources | <25% success rate |
| **Funding gap** | Pre-negotiated bridge financing | Cash <6 months operating |
| **Customer concentration** | No customer >10% of revenue | Ongoing monitoring |
| **Currency fluctuations** | Hedge EUR/USD exposure | >20% revenue in USD |
| **Hardware failure** | RAID, hot spares, 2 datacenters | Any single point of failure |
| **Colocation issues** | Dual datacenter (FR+DE) | Any single location failure |
