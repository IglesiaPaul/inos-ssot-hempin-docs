# Team Structures & Hiring Plan

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01  
**Selected Scenario:** Hybrid (Scenario B)

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the team structure evolution from bootstrap (1 founder) to full scale (100+ people). The Hybrid scenario was selected, balancing human oversight with AI assistance for optimal cost, speed, and quality.

| Phase | Timeline | Team Size | Monthly Burn | Focus |
|-------|----------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| **Bootstrap** | Months 1-6 | 1 founder + AI | €0-5K | Documentation, grants, advisors |
| **MVP** | Months 7-12 | 5-8 people | €50K-80K | Technical prototype, Thailand pilot |
| **Expansion** | Year 2 | 15-20 people | €150K-200K | 5 countries, Hemp Swap launch |
| **Scale** | Year 3 | 30-40 people | €300K-400K | 15+ countries, NADA token |
| **Solarpunk** | Years 5-20 | 100+ people | €1M+ | Global infrastructure |

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Key Person Risk** | Documentation, cross-training, equity retention |
| **Cash Flow Risk** | 18-month runway target, grant diversification |
| **Culture Dilution** | Strong values, mission-first hiring, regular retreats |
| **Burnout** | 4-day weeks during bootstrap, mandatory time off |
| **Talent Competition** | Mission alignment, equity, remote flexibility |
| **Regulatory Compliance** | Legal review of all contracts, local employment law |
