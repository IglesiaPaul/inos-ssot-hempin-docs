## What Is Solarpunk?

Solarpunk is a movement, aesthetic, and philosophy envisioning a future where:

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| ✅ **Technology serves ecology** | Not extraction, but regeneration |
| ✅ **Energy is 100% renewable** | Distributed, community-owned |
| ✅ **Economies are regenerative** | Circular, not linear |
| ✅ **Communities are resilient** | Self-determining, localized |
| ✅ **Beauty and function unified** | Art + engineering + nature |
| ✅ **Justice and sustainability inseparable** | No climate action without equity |

### Solarpunk vs. Cyberpunk

| Aspect | Cyberpunk (Dystopian) | Solarpunk (Utopian) |
|--------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Technology** | Oppressive, corporate-controlled | Liberating, community-owned |
| **Environment** | Degraded, polluted | Restored, integrated |
| **Economy** | Extractive, monopolistic | Regenerative, distributed |
| **Society** | Fragmented, unequal | Connected, equitable |
| **Energy** | Fossil fuels, nuclear | Solar, wind, biological |
| **Aesthetic** | Neon, rain, decay | Green, light, growth |

**Our Role:** INOS/SSOT is the **verification and coordination layer** that makes Solarpunk economics possible.
