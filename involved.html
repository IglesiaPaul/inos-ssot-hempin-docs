Frequently Asked Questions

What's the time commitment for advisors?

2-4 hours/month for strategic input, occasional intros, or grant strategy review. No deliverables required pre-funding. Equity vests over 2 years.

How does consortium participation work?

We sign a non-binding Letter of Intent (LOI) to join the EIC Pathfinder consortium. If the grant is awarded, we formalize roles and funding. No cost to partners during proposal phase.

Is this only for hemp?

Hemp is our proof-of-concept domain. The architecture generalizes to any supply chain requiring verified trust: organic agriculture, textiles, critical minerals, coffee, cocoa. By 2045, we envision planetary-scale verified infrastructure.

How is the Regeneration Fund governed?

30% of transaction value flows to verified environmental cleanup projects. Governance is community-driven via the INOS Foundation (non-profit), ensuring mission lock and transparency.

Can I contribute if I'm not in the hemp industry?

Absolutely. We welcome collaborators from sustainability, AI, regulatory science, economics, and social impact. The Solarpunk 2045 vision requires diverse expertise.
