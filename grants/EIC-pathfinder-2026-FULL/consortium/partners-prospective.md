## 🔧 How to Update Excel Budget When Partners Are Confirmed

**Step-by-Step Guide for Adding a Confirmed Partner:**

1.  **Open the Excel template** (`Tpl_Detailed Budget Table (HE Lump Sum).xlsm`)
2.  **Go to `BE list` sheet**:
    - Click "Add BE" button
    - Fill: BE nr (auto), Role (Beneficiary), BE name, Acronym, Country (dropdown), Funding rate (100% for RIA)
    - Click "Apply changes" → This auto-generates a new `BEx` sheet (e.g., BE2)
3.  **Fill the new `BEx` sheet**:
    - Enter person-months in Section A.1 (Senior/Junior scientists) for relevant WPs
    - Enter subcontracting costs in Section B (if applicable)
    - Enter travel/equipment/other costs in Section C
    - Leave Section D blank unless topic-specific costs apply
    - Section E (Indirect costs) auto-calculates at 25% of (A+C)
4.  **Update `Depreciation costs` sheet** (if equipment >15% of personnel):
    - Enter purchase cost, % used for project, % useful life in project
    - Calculate charged depreciation (manual entry in `BEx` sheet under C.2 Equipment)
5.  **Justify high costs in `Any comments` sheet**:
    - Example: "Personnel costs for senior scientists (€4,200/PM) reflect Belgian market rates for regulatory experts; aligned with Horizon Dashboard benchmarks."
6.  **Update Part A online form**:
    - Enter the new beneficiary's requested grant amount (from `Lump sum breakdown` sheet total)
    - Ensure total requested grant matches Excel `Lump sum breakdown` total
7.  **Save and export**:
    - Save as `.xlsm` for your records
    - Export as `.xlsx` for submission (macros disabled for security)

**Critical Consistency Checks:**
- [ ] Person-months in Excel `BEx` sheet = Part B Table 3.1f
- [ ] Equipment costs >15% of personnel justified in `Any comments` sheet
- [ ] Total grant request in Part A = Excel `Lump sum breakdown` total
- [ ] WP names in Excel `WP list` = Part B Table 3.1a (exact match)

## ⚠️ Submission Rules for Unconfirmed Partners

1.  **Do NOT list unconfirmed partners in Part A** of the proposal (online form) — only confirmed beneficiaries with PIC numbers.
2.  **Do NOT include unconfirmed partner costs in the Excel budget table** — only include costs for confirmed beneficiaries.
3.  **DO describe prospective partners in Part B Section 3.2** — evaluators understand consortia are built during proposal preparation. Use language like: "We are in advanced discussions with [Organization] for participation in WP[X]; final confirmation pending LOI signature."
4.  **DO attach LOIs as annexes if available** — strengthens credibility but is not mandatory for submission.
5.  **DO be transparent about status** — "Partners in discussion" is acceptable; "Partners confirmed" is not if LOIs are not signed.

**Reference:** EIC Work Programme 2026, Section B.3: "Consortium composition may evolve during proposal preparation; final composition must be validated at grant signature."
