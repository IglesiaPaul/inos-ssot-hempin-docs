# 1. Excellence

## 1.1 Long-term vision

**The Vision: Solarpunk 2045 — Verified Truth Infrastructure for Planetary Regeneration**

By 2045, global supply chains will operate on a layer of verified truth infrastructure that enables efficient cross-border trade while automatically enforcing regulatory compliance and funding environmental regeneration. This infrastructure will not be owned by any single corporation or state but will operate as a sovereign, open protocol — akin to TCP/IP for physical goods. The INOS/SSOT/HEMPIN project is the foundational research phase toward realizing this vision, starting with the hemp industry as a proof-of-concept domain.

**The Radically New Technology: Regulatory-Encoding Knowledge Graphs**

Current supply chain technologies (blockchain, ERP, traceability platforms) focus on recording transactions after they occur. They are backward-looking, siloed, and unable to encode complex regulatory logic across multiple jurisdictions. The radically new technology envisioned here is a **multi-dimensional knowledge graph with embedded regulatory science**.

This technology converges three distinct fields:
1.  **Graph Theory & Event Sourcing:** A single source of truth (SSOT) that encodes entities (farmers, processors, brands), relationships (trades, contracts), and value flows as immutable, time-series events.
2.  **Regulatory Science as Code:** Machine-readable encoding of 57+ jurisdictional regulations (THC limits, licensing, labeling) directly into the graph schema, enabling real-time compliance validation rather than post-hoc auditing.
3.  **AI-Native Onboarding (BUD Agent):** A natural-language-to-graph pipeline that allows non-technical actors (smallholder farmers) to verify onto the network via conversational AI, reducing onboarding friction from weeks to minutes.

This convergence creates a **Compliance Engine** that is proactive rather than reactive. Instead of discovering non-compliance at customs, the system prevents non-compliant transactions from being recorded in the first place. This is a paradigm shift from "trust but verify" to "verify by design."

**Long-Term Transformative Effects**

If achieved, this technology will have transformative positive effects on the economy, environment, and society beyond the project duration:

*   **Economy (Efficient Trade):** By reducing coordination failure in fragmented markets (starting with the $17B hemp industry), the technology enables efficient cross-border trade without manual compliance checks. Long-term, this reduces transaction costs by an estimated 30-40% for SMEs, unlocking access to global markets currently blocked by regulatory complexity. The technology scales beyond hemp to organic agriculture, textiles, and critical minerals by 2035.
*   **Environment (Regenerative Economics):** The architecture includes a **Regeneration Fund protocol** where 30% of transaction fees are automatically routed to verified environmental cleanup projects (soil remediation, water restoration). This embeds regeneration into the economic layer of supply chains, creating a sustainable funding mechanism for planetary repair independent of philanthropy or government grants.
*   **Society (Data Sovereignty):** By building on a sovereign EU technology stack (OVHcloud, Memgraph, Mistral AI), the infrastructure ensures data never leaves EU jurisdiction, reducing exposure to extraterritorial laws (e.g., US Cloud Act). This empowers European SMEs with data sovereignty, ensuring they control their commercial relationships rather than relying on US-based platforms.

**Project Contribution to the Vision**

The INOS/SSOT/HEMPIN project (48 months) is the **proof-of-principle research phase** (TRL 2-4) toward this 2045 vision. It does not aim to deploy the full global infrastructure but to validate the scientific and technological basis:

1.  **Validate Graph Schema:** Prove that regulatory rules can be encoded as graph constraints without losing flexibility for local variations (WP2, WP4).
2.  **Validate AI Onboarding:** Prove that natural language processing can accurately extract verified entities from unstructured farmer data with >85% confidence (WP3).
3.  **Validate Sovereign Stack:** Prove that EU-based infrastructure can handle the latency and throughput requirements for real-time compliance validation (WP2).
4.  **Validate Pilot Model:** Prove that actors will onboard to a verified network when compliance friction is removed (WP5, Thailand + EU pilots).

Successful completion of this project will de-risk the technology for subsequent Horizon Europe Innovation Actions (EIC Accelerator, Horizon Europe Cluster 4/6) and private investment, paving the way for full deployment by 2030. The hemp industry is the ideal testbed: high regulatory fragmentation, strong sustainability mandate, and fragmented SME landscape. Once proven in hemp, the architecture generalizes to other supply chains requiring verified trust.

**Alignment with EU Strategic Priorities**

This vision directly supports the EU's Strategic Autonomy (sovereign tech stack), Green Deal (regenerative funding), and Digital Decade (AI adoption in SMEs). It positions Europe as the leader in verified supply chain infrastructure, preventing dependency on US or Chinese platforms for critical trade data.
