# Consortium Targets for EIC Pathfinder Open 2026

## Eligibility Requirements
- **Minimum 3 legal entities**, independent from each other.
- **Each established in a different country**.
- **At least 1 legal entity established in a Member State** (EU).
- **At least 2 other independent legal entities**, each established in different Member States or Associated Countries.
