## Notes & Lessons Learned

**What Works:**
- Personalized emails (research their background)
- Clear time commitment (2-4 hours/month)
- Specific value ask (not vague)
- Mission alignment (Solarpunk 2045)
- Equity + purpose (not just equity)

**What Doesnt Work:**
- Generic copy-paste emails
- Vague time expectations
- Unclear value proposition
- No documentation provided
- Pressure for immediate decision

**Iterate Based On:**
- Response rates by advisor type
- Conversion rates by outreach template
- Time to commitment
- Advisor satisfaction scores
