## General Tips for Advisor Outreach

### Best Practices

| Do | Dont |
|----|------|
| Personalize each email (research their background) | Send generic copy-paste emails |
| Be specific about what you want from them | Be vague about time commitment |
| Provide clear documentation links | Expect them to research on their own |
| Offer 3 specific time slots | Say "let me know when you are free" |
| Follow up once (7 days later) | Give up after no response |
| Respect their time (keep calls to 30 min) | Let calls run over an hour |

### Response Rate Optimization

| Tactic | Expected Improvement |
|--------|---------------------|
| Personalized subject line | +20% open rate |
| Mutual connection mention | +30% response rate |
| Clear value proposition | +25% interest |
| Specific time slots | +15% meeting booked |
| Follow-up email | +40% overall response |

### Tracking Responses

| Outcome | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Positive response | Schedule call, send calendar invite |
| Neutral/maybe | Send more info, follow up in 1 week |
| No response | Send follow-up at day 7 |
| Declined | Thank them, ask for referrals |
| Referred to someone else | Thank them, contact referral |
