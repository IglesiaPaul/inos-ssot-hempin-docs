## Presentation Tips

### Before the Presentation

- Research your audience (investors vs. grants vs. partners)
- Customize the deck (emphasize relevant slides)
- Practice timing (15-20 minutes max)
- Prepare for Q&A (common questions below)
- Test technology (projector, clicker, video)

### During the Presentation

- Start with the problem (emotional connection)
- Tell a story (not just facts)
- Make eye contact (engage the audience)
- Pause for questions (check understanding)
- End with the vision (inspire action)

### After the Presentation

- Send follow-up within 24 hours
- Include deck + one-pager
- Answer any unanswered questions
- Schedule next meeting
- Add to CRM/tracker

## Common Q&A

**Q: Why hemp first?**
A: Hemp is the perfect beachhead: high regulatory complexity, strong association networks, growing market, proof of concept for multi-industry expansion.

**Q: What prevents Metrc or IBM from copying this?**
A: Temporal moat. You cannot buy three years of verified relationship data. We earn it one entity, one association, one trade at a time.

**Q: How do you ensure mission alignment with investors?**
A: Foundation + OpCo structure, mission lock mechanisms, B-Corp certification, investor selection criteria.

**Q: What is your grant success rate assumption?**
A: 25% average (industry average is 15%). We achieve this through volume (500+ sources), quality (expert advisors), and diversification (EU, UN, national, foundations).

**Q: How do you handle regulatory changes?**
A: Compliance layer is continuously updated through Hemp Commons feeds. 57 jurisdiction diversification reduces single-market risk.

**Q: What is your biggest risk?**
A: Execution risk (can we build this?). Mitigated through experienced team, phased roadmap, and advisor support.

**Q: Why should I invest now?**
A: You are getting in at the ground floor of critical infrastructure for planetary regeneration. The temporal moat means early participants capture disproportionate value.
