## Decision-Making Framework

When facing any decision:

**Step 1: Check Existing Documentation**
- Is this already decided? (feedback/open-questions.md)
- Is there an ADR? (decisions/ folder)
- What does STRATEGY.md say?

**Step 2: Apply Decision Framework**
- Does this advance Solarpunk 2045 vision?
- Does this maintain sovereignty (no US dependency)?
- Does this increase or decrease regeneration?
- Does this center Global South voices?
- Is this transparent and accountable?

**Step 3: Document the Decision**
- If major: Create new ADR (decisions/ADR-XXX.md)
- If minor: Update open-questions.md
- Always: Update relevant documentation

**Step 4: Communicate**
- Update team (Slack, email, meeting)
- Update documentation
- Update trackers (advisor-tracker.md, grant-application-tracker.md)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the single most important metric?**
A: Verified entities onboarded. Everything else follows from graph density.

**Q: What is the biggest risk?**
A: Execution risk (can we build this?). Mitigated through phased roadmap and advisor support.

**Q: What happens if grants dont come through?**
A: Pivot to private capital (€5M seed) or activate pivot strategies (strategy/pivot-strategies.md).

**Q: Why hemp first?**
A: Perfect beachhead: high regulatory complexity, strong association networks, growing market, proof of concept for multi-industry expansion.

**Q: What prevents copying?**
A: Temporal moat. You cannot buy three years of verified relationship data. We earn it one entity at a time.

**Q: How do I contribute?**
A: Contact founder. Opportunities: advisors, grants, technical, partnerships, talent.
