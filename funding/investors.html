Why This Is Defensible

🔐

Regulatory-Encoding Knowledge Graphs

Not just traceability — active prevention of non-compliant transactions via graph constraints. 57 jurisdictions encoded as machine-readable rules (SHACL/Cypher). No existing platform combines regulatory science + graph theory at this scale.

🏢

Physical-Digital Hybrid Moat

Country showrooms (~$15K fit-out + $3-6K/mo ops) are expensive to replicate. Each showroom is a trust node, sales office, and product showcase. Software alone cannot replicate this physical presence + local relationship building.

🇪🇺

Sovereign AI Stack

Full EU sovereignty: no US Cloud Act exposure for sensitive commercial data. Self-hosted Mistral 7B on OVHcloud — validated performance benchmark. Strategic alignment with EU Digital Decade + Strategic Autonomy priorities.

⏳

Temporal Moat: Graph Density

"You cannot buy three years of verified, relationship-dense, event-sourced graph data. You have to earn it." First 10,000 verified entities across 15 countries create an authoritative data layer. Marginal value accelerates with density.

💧

Mission-Locked Economics

30% of transaction value automatically routes to Regeneration Fund — encoded in architecture, not policy. Foundation + OpCo structure prevents mission drift if acquired. Attracts impact capital aligned with long-term regeneration.

🌱

Hemp as the Hardest Test Case

If verified truth infrastructure works for hemp — the most fragmented, stigmatized, regulated plant-based industry — it works for any regenerative plant-based industry. Hemp is the prototype. INOS is the pattern.
