How the Fund Works

Unlike traditional CSR or carbon offset programs, the Regeneration Fund is embedded in the architecture — not an add-on, not optional, not dependent on corporate goodwill.

Transaction Flow

€1,000 Transaction

Hemp trade on HEMPIN

→

Automatic Split

Encoded in smart contract

→

€700 to Supplier

70% of transaction value

→

€300 to Fund

30% to verified cleanup

Mission-Locked by Design

The 30% allocation is encoded in the Value Layer of the SSOT graph. It cannot be changed without community governance approval. This ensures regeneration continues even if leadership changes or the company is acquired.

Impact Areas

The Regeneration Fund supports verified environmental cleanup projects aligned with planetary regeneration goals.

🌱

Soil Remediation

Restoring degraded agricultural land through regenerative farming practices and phytoremediation.

💧

Water Restoration

Cleaning contaminated waterways, restoring wetlands, and protecting freshwater ecosystems.

🌳

Carbon Sequestration

Supporting reforestation, soil carbon capture, and blue carbon projects (mangroves, seagrass).

♻️

Waste Cleanup

Removing plastic pollution, agricultural waste, and industrial contaminants from ecosystems.
