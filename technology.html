Why Sovereignty Matters

Building on EU infrastructure isn't just a technical choice — it's a strategic necessity:

  • Data Sovereignty: Ensures European SMEs control their commercial relationships, not US platforms
  • Regulatory Compliance: GDPR-by-design with data minimization and explicit consent flows
  • Strategic Autonomy: Reduces exposure to extraterritorial laws (US Cloud Act) for critical trade infrastructure
  • Mission Alignment: Sovereign tech enables the Solarpunk 2045 vision of decentralized, community-governed infrastructure
