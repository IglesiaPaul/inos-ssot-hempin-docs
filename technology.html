Community tools for efficient inference. Ollama simplifies model management; vLLM provides high-throughput serving with PagedAttention.

German-built framework for RAG pipelines. Orchestrates retrieval, generation, and validation in the BUD Agent workflow.

🛡️ AI Robustness Measures (EIC Requirement)

  • Technical Robustness: Confidence scoring (>0.85 threshold) + human-in-the-loop validation for low-confidence extractions
  • Social Robustness: Training data diversified across languages (EN, FR, TH, RO) to reduce bias; human review for sensitive regulatory decisions
  • Reliability: Service-level agreements (99.9% uptime); error logging and incident response protocols
  • Explainability: Each graph node includes provenance trail (source text, confidence score, reviewer ID) for auditability

⚖️ The Trade-offs: Honesty About Sovereignty

Building sovereign isn't always easier. Here's what we gave up — and why it was worth it.

Smaller Community

Trade-off: Some tools have smaller communities than US alternatives

Benefit: Community governance, not vendor control

We actively contribute to open source projects to help them grow. Our participation shapes the future of these tools.

More Ops Overhead

Trade-off: Self-hosting requires more operational expertise

Benefit: Full control, no vendor lock-in

We invest in documentation, automation, and training to make self-hosting sustainable. The control is worth the effort.

GPU Hardware Reality

Trade-off: High-performance GPUs still come from US companies (NVIDIA)

Benefit: Self-hosting + model optimization reduces dependency

We optimize models to run on fewer GPUs, support open GPU initiatives, and review alternatives annually.
