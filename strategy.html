Strategic Scenarios & Pivot Options

We've modeled multiple pathways based on funding outcomes and market conditions. Each scenario maintains our core mission while adapting to resource availability.

Bootstrap Path (No External Funding)

  • Timeline: 3-5 years to first revenue
  • Team Size: 1-3 people (founder + contractors)
  • Scope: Single-country pilot (Thailand), minimal AI features
  • Cost: ~€200K over 2 years
  • Revenue Model: SaaS fees from early adopters
  • Risk Mitigation: Focus on core SSOT architecture; defer advanced AI features

Grant-Funded Path (EIC Pathfinder Success)

  • Timeline: 48 months (2026-2030)
  • Team Size: 8-12 people (consortium)
  • Scope: Multi-country pilots, full SSOT/INOS/HEMPIN stack
  • Cost: €4M (100% grant-funded)
  • Revenue Model: Post-grant commercialization via OpCo
  • Risk Mitigation: Consortium diversification; milestone-based funding

Hybrid Path (Mixed Funding)

  • Timeline: 2-3 years to product-market fit
  • Team Size: 4-6 people
  • Scope: Thailand pilot + lightweight EU pilot, core features only
  • Cost: €1M (€500K grant, €500K private)
  • Revenue Model: Pilot revenue + follow-on grants
  • Risk Mitigation: Phased feature rollout; revenue validation before scaling
