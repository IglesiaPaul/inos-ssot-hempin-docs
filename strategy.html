Strategy

Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-01 | Status: Draft

The Problem: The global hemp industry is a coordination failure at scale: 57 jurisdictions with incompatible regulations, no shared identity standard, no cross-border provenance, no verified trust layer. Result: $17B market cannot trade efficiently.

The Solution

SSOT (Single Source of Truth)

A continuously updating, multi-dimensional knowledge graph — a living representation of the hemp economy at:

  • Entity layer: Canonical representation of every verified actor
  • Relationship layer: Every interaction between entities
  • Compliance layer: Per-jurisdiction regulatory encoding
  • Value layer: Economic flow graph (NADA, GMV, fees)

INOS (Operating System)

The architecture that produces SSOT: Microservices + Event Sourcing + Graph Database + Sovereign infrastructure.

HEMPIN (First Deployment)

The application layer: Hemp Commons (regulatory Q&A), Hemp Swap (verified trading), Hemp Globe (geospatial map), BUD AI Agent (natural language onboarding).
