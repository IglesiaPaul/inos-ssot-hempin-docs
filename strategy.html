Strategy

Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-04 | Status: Active

The Problem: The global hemp industry is a coordination failure at scale: 57 jurisdictions with incompatible regulations, no shared identity standard, no cross-border provenance, no verified trust layer. Result: $17B market cannot trade efficiently.

The Solution

SSOT (Single Source of Truth)

A continuously updating, multi-dimensional knowledge graph — a living representation of the hemp economy at:

  • Entity layer: Canonical representation of every verified actor
  • Relationship layer: Every interaction between entities
  • Compliance layer: Per-jurisdiction regulatory encoding as machine-readable constraints
  • Value layer: Economic flow graph with embedded Regeneration Fund (30% to environmental cleanup)

INOS (Operating System)

The architecture that produces SSOT: Microservices + Event Sourcing + Graph Database + Sovereign EU infrastructure (OVHcloud, Memgraph, Mistral AI). Zero US dependency.

HEMPIN (First Deployment)

The application layer: Hemp Commons (regulatory Q&A), Hemp Swap (verified trading), Hemp Globe (geospatial map), BUD AI Agent (natural language onboarding with confidence scoring).

🔬 Core Scientific Breakthroughs:
  • Natural-Language-to-Graph (NL2G): BUD AI converts farmer conversations into verified graph nodes with >85% confidence scoring
  • Regulatory Ontology as Code: Machine-readable encoding of 57 jurisdictions as graph constraints (SHACL/Cypher) for real-time compliance validation
  • Sovereign AI Stack: Self-hosted Mistral 7B on EU cloud infrastructure, eliminating US API dependency for sensitive commercial data
